I am a PhD candidate at the Microeconomics department at Maastricht University. My advisers are Elias Tsakas , Martin Strobel and Stefan Terstiege .

In my research, I am primarily interested in the demand and supply of information as well as biases with information processing. Specifically, I currently investigate the influence of misinformation and verifications on information processing. In another project I study information elicitation in a principal-agent setting.

Currently, I am a visiting researcher at the Johnson Graduate School of Management at Cornell University working with Collin Raymond.
